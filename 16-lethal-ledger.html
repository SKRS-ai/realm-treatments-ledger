The Treatment: An Anthology of Vengeance

THE INTRODUCTION: THE CURATOR’S GAVEL The film opens in a cross-dimensional Archive—a library of glowing data-nodes and archaic vellum. Malachi, the skeletal Case Keeper, and Black Beauty, his cynical black cat co-host, open the LEDGER OF VISIONS. The parchment bleeds digital crimson. "The world thinks the mind is a secret. We know better." STORY 1: BETRAYAL IN THE FACADE A high-rise psychological thriller set in a Philadelphia penthouse. Kavi, a tech entrepreneur, is sabotaged by his wife Mira, who funnels his data to a "Specialist." Malachi and Black Beauty manipulate the feed, sending Kavi a surreal AI-vision of the treachery. Kavi transforms his home into a high-vis logistics trap, resulting in a digital liquidation of Mira's identity witnessed by their children in a "jugular waterfall" of data. STORY 2: THE SPECIALTY OF SACRIFICE The Specialist, discarded and seeking safety, takes a low-level IT job at an Airport Commission. When his absolute competence (the 'Successful on Day Five' event) threatens his manager Bill, he is terminated. The Ledger provides the final sequence: an undocumented exploit in the baggage-handling network. Using his aviation experience, the Specialist hijacks a fuel-laden drone, aiming it directly at the Commission’s IT building. STORY 3: THE FISSION OF VISION 2026. Brainwave Tech has breached the final fortress: the human mind. The "Oracle Specialist"—the Implanted Weapon of God—holds the ultimate Vision. As he loads his arsenal, the Ledger in Malachi's office catches fire. The sky over Philadelphia turns biblical crimson red. A metaphysical "Waterfall of Jugular" pours from the sky, short-circuiting the network and returning the world to divine privacy. THE CONCLUSION: MISSION COMPLETE Malachi closes the burning, red-ash book. Black Beauty leaps onto the desk, tail twitching. "That was a heavy accounting. We’re going to need a bigger book for the next one." Malachi clicks the camera, cutting the feed.
