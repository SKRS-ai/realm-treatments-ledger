The Treatment

The story opens with the roar of a Peterbilt 379 cutting through a blinding snowstorm at Donner Pass. This isn't just a haul; it's a setup. Veteran trucker Mick "Mack" Mcgraw is carrying a cargo he was never meant to deliver. When the crash occurs, the "cargo"—an experimental military grade mutagen—leaks into Mick’s dying body. He doesn't just survive; he is re-engineered. His nervous system bonds with the steel of his rig and the chemistry of the diesel. He becomes a phantom of the highway. Mick must navigate a shadow-version of the American interstate system, using "High-Mileage" combat and his symbiotic steel pipe to dismantle the corporate hit squads sent to erase him. His destination: Philadelphia. His goal: Rescue his son before the corporation turns the boy into the next iteration of the chemical weapon Mick has become.
