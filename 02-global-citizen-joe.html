JOE - TRANS-ATLANTIC NETWORK HUB

The Treatment

Joe is a man caught between two worlds: the analog rumble of the highway and the silent, fiber-optic pulse of the sea. Starting in the North Philadelphia neighborhoods that raised him, Joe seeks an escape from the physical limits of the road. He secures a position as a Senior Systems Engineer on a luxury global liner. The transition isn't just professional—it's spiritual. As he troubleshoots the ship's complex "nervous system" while crossing the equator, Joe begins to see the world not as a map of borders, but as a network of human signals. The story follows Joe through vibrant ports of call, from the tech hubs of Tokyo to the coastal rhythms of Brazil. However, when a massive system failure threatens the ship’s navigation in the middle of a storm, Joe must use his "old world" trucker intuition and his "new world" IT expertise to save the thousands of souls on board. It is here he realizes that being a "Global Citizen" isn't about where you go, but the connections you maintain.
