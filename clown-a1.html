DEADHEAD

"After a fatal crash at Donner Pass, a veteran trucker is resurrected by a classified military chemical into an indestructible force of nature—fueled by diesel and armed with a symbiotic steel pipe, he must now 'leap' across the country to rescue his son from the ruthless corporation that staged his death."

SYSTEM REBOOT // SUBJECT: DALTON

The Treatment

The story opens with the roar of a Peterbilt 379 cutting through a blinding snowstorm at Donner Pass. This isn't just a haul; it's a setup. Veteran trucker Jack "Deadhead" Dalton is carrying a cargo he was never meant to deliver. When the crash occurs, a classified military mutagen leaks into Jack’s dying body. He doesn't just survive; he is re-engineered at the molecular level. His nervous system bonds with the steel of his rig and the volatile chemistry of diesel fuel. He becomes a phantom of the high-mileage road. Jack must navigate a shadow-version of the American interstate system, using his symbiotic steel pipe to dismantle corporate hit squads. His destination is Philadelphia, where his son is being held. To save him, Jack must embrace the monster he has become and behead the digital monster before they drive him to a breaking point.
